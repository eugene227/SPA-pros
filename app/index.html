Collaboration Starts Here

Impacting team workflow by providing the tools

needed to share feedback with peers within an

online survey platform

MOBILE FRIENDLY

Build with mobile first design that is responsive to a wide range of modern devices.

TONE ANALYZER

Get instant feedback of the writing tone you use in any of your questions or responces before sending them out.

ACCESSIBLE

Provides easy navigation for keyboard-only users and those who are visually impaired by color blindness.

How to start sharing feedback with your team

1

SET UP A SURVEY

Write up your own questions for peer-reviews, with the option to save rough drafts before promoting.

• • • • • • • • • • • • • •

2

LAUNCH AND PROMOTE

Once you're satisfied with the final draft, share your new survey for feedback.

• • • • • • • • • • • • • •

3

CHECK EACH OTHERS FEEDBACK

Review survey and give feedback with our simple platform, with the option of hiding the reviewers name.
